Containment of Communism was the guiding principle of United States foreign policy for more than 40 years. The Big Bend of the Rio Grande has been outlaw country for a long time, and now it is drug smugglers who increasingly find the terrain a useful ally. A12 REPORT ON C.I.A. ANALYSES

A C.I.A. task force appointed in the aftermath of Robert M. Gates's grueling confirmation hearings last fall has rejected the assertion that the agency systematically slanted intelligence over the years. The findings, included in a classified report recently submitted to Mr. Gates for his approval, largely blame poor management and the inexperience of some analysts for allowing the perception of purposely slanted intelligence to flourish.
